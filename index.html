Swapify

swapify.netlify.com

The goal of Swapify is to help students save money. Students already spend thousands of dollars on tuition, they shouldn't have to spend hundreds more on text books when they can just swap them with their classmates on campus. Swapify facilitates this for the user. A user can post their text books that they want to swap and search all available text books to swap for. Users interact by sending a message to the owner of a book that they would like to swap for.

BookEx

tstirtz.github.io/BookEx

BookEx stemmed from my passion for learning and reading. The user can input a book that they enjoyed and BookEx will generate a list of books similar to the user input. The user can then click on one of the related results to be taken to a synopsis of the book and a list of used copies of the book being sole on amazon. The user can click buy on any of the listings to be taken directly to amazon with the book already placed in their shopping cart.
